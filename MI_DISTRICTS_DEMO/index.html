    • Racial Comp by School District in MI

    During the 2015-16 school year, 67 percent of students in Michigan's K-12 public schools were white, 18 percent were African-American, 7 percent were Hispanic, 3 percent were Asian, 3 percent were multi-race and less than 1 percent were Native American.
